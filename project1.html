Capstone Project: Augmented Reality Learning App

For my capstone project, my team and I developed an Augmented Reality (AR) Learning Application using Unity Engine and Vuforia AR. The main goal of the project was to enhance learning experiences by allowing users to interact with 3D models and animations through mobile devices in real-time.

The app recognizes image targets and displays interactive 3D content, including educational materials, quizzes, and visual demonstrations. This project helped us apply our skills in C# programming, Unity development, and AR integration, while also emphasizing user experience and accessibility.
